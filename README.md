# Privacy Policy — iPod Emulator - Music Player
Effective date: July 11, 2026

iPod Emulator - Music Player ("the app") is an offline music player for Android that plays audio files already stored on your device. This policy explains what information the app can access, how it is used, and what happens to it. The short version: iPod Emulator - Music Player does not collect, transmit, share, or sell any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Data the app does NOT collect
iPod Emulator - Music Player has no internet access and contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind. The app does not collect or transmit:

Personal information (name, email address, phone number, contacts)
Location data
Device identifiers
Usage or diagnostic analytics
Your music files, playlists, or listening history
No data leaves your device, because the app does not request the network (INTERNET) permission at all.

2. Information the app accesses on your device
To function as a music player, iPod Emulator - Music Player reads the following locally, on your device only:

Music library (audio files permission). The app uses Android's READ_MEDIA_AUDIO permission (or READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE on Android 12 and below) to list and play the songs, albums, artists, and album artwork already stored on your device. This information is read from Android's media library and is never uploaded, copied elsewhere, or shared.
App settings and playback state. Preferences you choose in the app (for example clicker sound, haptics, shuffle, repeat) and your current playback queue are saved in the app's private storage on your device so they can be restored the next time you open the app.
3. Other permissions the app uses
Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) — shows the standard media playback notification with play/pause and skip controls.
Foreground service (FOREGROUND_SERVICE, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK) — keeps music playing while the app is in the background.
Wake lock (WAKE_LOCK) — prevents the device from interrupting audio playback when the screen turns off.
None of these permissions involve collecting or sharing data.

4. Data sharing and third parties
iPod Emulator - Music Player does not share any data with third parties, because it does not collect any. The app contains no third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or social media integrations.

5. Data retention and deletion
The only data the app stores are your local settings and playback queue, kept in the app's private storage. Uninstalling the app, or using Settings > Apps > iPod Emulator - Music Player > Clear data on your device, permanently deletes all of it. Your music files themselves are never modified or deleted by the app.

If Android's automatic backup is enabled on your device, the app's settings may be included in your device backup handled by Google (subject to Google's privacy policy). This backup is controlled by your device settings, not by the app.

6. Children's privacy
iPod Emulator - Music Player is suitable for general audiences and does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.
